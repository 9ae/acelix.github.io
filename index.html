Deja Vu

Deja Vu for iOS enhances your video experience to help you quickly find those memorable past times. We achieve this by accessing various voice/image/facial recognition services, ranging from REST-ful APIs to open-source libraries. Facebook friends' faces are machine recognized, their words captioned, and even simple objects identified to provide search terms. Think of it as a personal search engine for everything in your phone. This project is one of the top four finalist of Kaltura's VideoXHackathon and winner of the VoiceBase prize
